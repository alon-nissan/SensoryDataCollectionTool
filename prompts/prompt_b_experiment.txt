## Key Notes

- ALWAYS check for multiple experiments. Look for language like "Experiment 1", "Study 1", "Phase 1", "Session A/B", or distinct experimental conditions with different stimuli sets.
- Include ALL session details mentioned — even seemingly minor details like cup size, sample temperature, or compensation.
- For `design_type`, look for mentions of balanced designs, Latin squares, or randomization schemes.
- If a detail is not mentioned in the paper, set it to null — do NOT fabricate values.
